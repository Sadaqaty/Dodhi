OUR MISSION

Blending Heritage & Tech

We believe technology should serve cultural values. Dodhi preserves the traditional trust of Pakistani dairy routes while offering state-of-the-art offline engineering.

Preserving the Trust system

In Pakistan, millions rely on local milkmen (known as *Dodhis*) who visit houses daily. For decades, transactions have been recorded on small paper slips (*Parchis*) or wall scratchings. However, paper slips easily get wet, lost, or torn.

Dodhi was created to modernize this ledger system without introducing unnecessary complexity. By utilizing a "default delivery" single-tap mechanism, milkmen don't have to deal with complicated keyboard inputs while riding their routes.

With support for localized languages, Roman Urdu text reports, and customizable rate structures, Dodhi honors local traditions while providing professional-grade accounting and billing tools.

صاف حساب، لمبی یاری
(Clean ledger, long partnership)

"Our motto is rooted in the traditional wisdom of Pakistani local business. Trust is built on clear accounting. By generating instant reports and ledger files in Urdu, Dodhi establishes crystal-clear transparency between milkmen and households, ensuring a lifetime of friendly relationships."
