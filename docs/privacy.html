Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

1. Overview

Welcome to **Dodhi**, a digital ledger application developed by **Fixare Studio** designed specifically for traditional milkmen to record and manage customer deliveries, payments, and balances.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy details how we handle information in the Dodhi app.

2. Offline-First & Zero Data Collection

**Dodhi is an offline-first mobile application.**

All records, customer details, milk deliveries, payments, custom rates, and settings are stored locally on your device in a secure SQLite database (via Android Room Database).

  • We **do not** host external servers, databases, or cloud sync systems.
  • We **do not** collect, store, or transmit your personal data or your customers' data.
  • All financial figures and ledger data remain entirely on your device and are never shared with us or any third parties.

3. Data Backups & File Sharing

The application provides a feature allowing you to export your data as a backup JSON file (`dodhi_backup_[timestamp].json`).

  • This backup file is generated locally on your device.
  • You retain complete ownership and control of this file. It is only shared or uploaded when you explicitly choose to do so using the Android share sheet (e.g. exporting to your own Google Drive, Dropbox, or sending via WhatsApp).
  • Importing a backup file reads data solely from the local file you select. No network connection is utilized.

4. Device Permissions

To provide essential features, the app requests the following system permissions:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+): Used to schedule daily entry reminders at 5:00 PM local time. These notifications are scheduled locally via Android's WorkManager; no external push server is used.
  • Storage/File Access: Required to write backup files to your designated document directories and read them during backup restorations.

5. Security

Since your data resides on your own mobile device, the security of your ledger relies on your device's security. We recommend setting a secure screen lock (PIN, pattern, or biometrics) on your phone to prevent unauthorized access to your records.

6. Children's Privacy

Our application does not collect any personal data, and is safe for use by individuals of all ages.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect modifications in application functionality or regulatory compliance. Any updates will be posted directly to our official website.
