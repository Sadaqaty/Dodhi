What Dodhi Does

Simple tools for daily delivery tracking, customer management, and financial reporting.

Daily Run

Tap Delivered or Naga for each customer. The app calculates bills automatically based on quantity and rate.

Customer Management

Add customers and suppliers with their daily quantity and rate per liter. Track who buys and who sells.

Payment Tracking

Record payments from customers and to suppliers. See who owes what at any time with clear balance view.

View profit, cash flow, and delivery volume. See your business health at a glance with color-coded cards.
