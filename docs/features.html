FEATURES

Everything a milkman needs

Simple, powerful tools designed specifically for daily dairy operations. No complexity, no unnecessary features.

One-Tap Daily Run

Open the daily run screen and tap Delivered or Naga for each customer. Default quantities are pre-filled based on each customer's usual amount. Complete your entire route in under a minute. No keyboards needed.

Business Reports Dashboard

See your monthly profit, sales vs purchases, cash flow (to collect vs to pay), milk volume chart, and active customer count — all on one screen. Color-coded cards make it easy to understand at a glance.

Customer Ledger & Payments

Tap any customer to see their complete history. View monthly bill, total liters, and outstanding balance. Record payments instantly. The app tracks who owes what, so you never lose count.

PDF & WhatsApp Reports

Generate professional PDF statements with monthly summaries, previous balance, and total payable. Or send a text summary directly to customers via WhatsApp in Urdu or English. Settle accounts in seconds.

🗣️

Bilingual (English & Urdu)

Every screen, button, and report works in both English and Urdu. The app adapts to your phone's language setting automatically.

💰

Custom Rates Per Customer

Set individual rates for each customer. The app captures the rate at the time of delivery, so old records stay accurate even if you change rates later.

🔔

Daily Reminders

A gentle notification at 5 PM reminds you to update your daily records. With fun Urdu and English messages to keep things light.

📱

Fully Offline

No internet required. All data stays on your phone in a secure local database. Works anywhere, even without signal.

🔄

Backup & Restore

Export your data as a JSON backup file. Restore it on any device. Automatic Google backup keeps your data safe.

🏪

Buy & Sell Tracking

Track both sides of your business — milk you buy from suppliers and milk you sell to customers. See profit from both.
