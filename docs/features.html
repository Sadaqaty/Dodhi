PREMIUM UTILITIES

Digital Registers Redefined

Every feature of Dodhi is engineered specifically to match the pacing and culture of traditional milkmen, providing maximum ease with minimal typing.

🚀

Zero-Keyboard Entries

Designed for milkmen on the move. Daily milk quantities default to the customer's typical amount. Log your entire route with simple taps in less than a minute.

🗣️

Bilingual English & Urdu

Form fields, button descriptions, and help menus adjust automatically to your system's selected language. Easy for village milkmen and city distributors alike.

💰

Custom Rate Sheets

Set personalized buffalo and cow milk rates per liter for every individual customer. The app automatically calculates delivery amounts based on customized rates.

🗒️

Digital Khata Ledgers

High-fidelity recording of daily deliveries, extra sales, and payment receipts. A unified database matches standard accounting rules automatically.

🔔

52+ Poetical Reminders

Receive notifications at 5:00 PM if entries are forgotten. Features charming Urdu/English poetry, local sayings, and lighthearted tea-time humor with custom vibrations.

📄

Urdu WhatsApp Parchi

Share monthly statements and itemized ledgers directly to customers' WhatsApp in clean, readable Roman-Urdu formats with Rs. calculation details.

🖨️

Cinematic PDF Export

Generate professionally styled PDF statements complete with monthly summaries, previous balance carryovers, payments received, and total payable amounts.

🔒

Offline & Private

Dodhi does not use cloud databases or remote servers. All your ledger statistics remain completely secure and offline in your local Android Room database.

🔄

JSON Backup & Restore

Export your ledger as a secure backup JSON file and save it to Google Drive or send it via email to transfer data between devices seamlessly.

SPOTLIGHT

Bilingual Dynamic Interface

Dodhi supports local needs. When creating customers or tracking milk quantities, the interface adapts to Urdu or English language modes seamlessly. Descriptions utilize resource strings to guide users without cluttering the premium interface.
