FEATURES

Everything a milkman needs

Simple, powerful tools designed specifically for daily dairy operations. No complexity, no unnecessary features.

One-Tap Daily Run

Open the daily run screen and tap Delivered or Naga for each customer. Default quantities are pre-filled based on each customer's usual amount. Complete your entire route in under a minute. No keyboards needed.

  • ✓ Default quantities per customer
  • ✓ Edit custom quantities when needed
  • ✓ Completed customers move to bottom
  • ✓ Progress bar shows daily completion

Customer Management

Add customers and suppliers with their name, locality, daily quantity, and rate per liter. Track who you sell to (Buyers) and who you buy from (Sellers). Each customer has their own rate and default quantity.

  • ✓ Separate buyers and sellers
  • ✓ Custom rate per customer
  • ✓ Locality tracking
  • ✓ Default daily quantity

Reports & Financial Overview

See your monthly profit, sales vs purchases, cash flow, delivery volume, and customer balances — all on one screen. Color-coded cards make it easy to understand at a glance.

  • ✓ Net profit with sales vs purchases
  • ✓ Cash flow: to collect and to pay
  • ✓ Daily volume chart for the month
  • ✓ Per-customer balance breakdown

Bilingual English & Urdu

Every screen, button, and report works in both English and Urdu. The app adapts to your phone's language setting automatically. Reports can be generated in either language for your customers.

  • ✓ Full UI in English and Urdu
  • ✓ Auto-detects phone language
  • ✓ Reports in Urdu or English
  • ✓ WhatsApp messages in Urdu
📄

PDF & WhatsApp Reports

Generate professional PDF statements or send text summaries directly to customers via WhatsApp in Urdu or English. Settle accounts in seconds.

💰

Payment Tracking

Record payments from customers and to suppliers. See who owes what at any time with clear balance view. Track both cash received and cash paid.

🔔

Daily Reminders

A gentle notification at 5 PM reminds you to update your daily records. With fun Urdu and English messages to keep things light.

📱

Fully Offline

No internet required. All data stays on your phone in a secure local database. Works anywhere, even without signal.

🔄

Backup & Restore

Export your data as a JSON backup file. Restore it on any device. Automatic Google backup keeps your data safe.

🏪

Buy & Sell Tracking

Track both sides of your business — milk you buy from suppliers and milk you sell to customers. See profit from both.
