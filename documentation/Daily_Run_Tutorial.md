# 🚚 Daily Run Tutorial

The **Daily Run** is where you spend most of your time. It’s designed to be fast and usable even while you're on the move.

## 1. Starting your Day

On the Dashboard, tap the **"Daily Run"** card. You will see a list of all your customers (both Providers and Consumers).

## 2. Marking Records

For each customer, you have two main actions:

### ✅ Delivered (Green Check)

- Tap the **Green Check** icon to mark the delivery.
- The app will use the **Standard Quantity** you set for that customer.
- **Variable Quantity**: If a customer takes more or less milk today, simply tap the quantity box and type the new amount _before_ hitting save.

### ❌ Naga (Red Cross)

- Tap the **Red Cross** icon if there was no delivery today.
- This creates a record with **0 quantity** and **0 cost**, ensuring your ledger stays chronological with no gaps.

## 3. Correcting Mistakes (Quick Edit)

If you marked a customer incorrectly:

1. Locate the customer in the list.
2. Tap the **Pencil (Edit)** icon next to their status (Delivered/Naga).
3. The row will reset, allowing you to re-enter the correct information.

## 4. Dashboard Quick Actions

You don't always need to go to the Daily Run screen! On the Dashboard, each customer card has a **"+" (Deliver)** button. Tapping this instantly records a delivery for today using their standard quantity.
