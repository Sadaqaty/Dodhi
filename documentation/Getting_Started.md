# 🐄 Getting Started with Dodhi

Welcome to **Dodhi**, your digital milk management accounting suite. This guide will help you set up your digital register in minutes.

## 1. Initial Setup

When you first open the app, tap the **Settings (Gear)** icon in the top right of the Dashboard.

- **Milkman Name**: Enter your name or business name. This will appear on all PDF reports you generate.
- **Language**: Choose between English and Urdu. The app will immediately switch all labels to your preferred language.

## 2. Understanding Customer Types

Dodhi handles two types of people in your business:

### 🥛 Providers (Buy)

These are the people **you buy milk from** (e.g., dairy farms or other milkmen).

- Marked with **Earth Brown** icons.
- When you "Add Customer", toggle the switch to **"Buy"**.

### 🥤 Consumers (Sell)

These are the people **you sell milk to** (e.g., households or shops).

- Marked with **Nature Green** icons.
- When you "Add Customer", toggle the switch to **"Sell"**.

## 3. Adding Your First Customer

1. Tap the **"Add Member"** card on the Dashboard.
2. Enter the **Name** and **Location**.
3. Set the **Rate per Liter**. This is the most important part—the app uses this to calculate all financial balances automatically.
4. Set the **Standard Quantity**. This is the amount they usually take every day. It will be pre-filled during your daily run to save you time.
5. Tap **"Save Member"**.
