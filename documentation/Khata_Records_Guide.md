# 📜 Khata Records & Balance Guide

Dodhi is more than just a delivery log—it’s a complete financial ledger (Khata). Here’s how the money works.

## 1. Automatic Balance Calculation

Every time you mark a delivery, the app calculates the cost based on the **Quantity** and the **Rate per Liter**.

- **Consumers (Customers)**: The balance increases when they take milk. A positive balance means **they owe you money**.
- **Providers (Dairy Farms)**: The balance increases when you take milk from them. A positive balance means **you owe them money**.

## 2. Managing Payments (Collecting / Giving)

When money changes hands, record it in the **Customer Detail** screen:

1. Tap on any customer from the Dashboard.
2. Tap the **"Add Payment"** button.
3. For a Consumer, enter the **Amount Collected**.
4. For a Provider, enter the **Amount Paid**.
5. The customer's balance will adjust immediately.

## 3. The Digital Ledger ("Parchi" Style)

Tap on **"Khata Records"** on the Dashboard to see your historical overview.

- **Chronological View**: Records are shown date-by-date, just like a traditional paper "Parchi".
- **Monthly Summaries**: View your total milk collection and distribution for each month.
- **Traditional Aesthetics**: The ledger uses a textured paper style and Earthy colors to feel familiar and professional.
