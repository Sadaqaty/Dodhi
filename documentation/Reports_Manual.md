# 📊 Reports & Sharing Manual

Sharing clear, professional reports with your customers builds trust and reduces disputes. Dodhi makes this instant.

## 1. Generating a Report

1. Go to any **Customer Detail** screen.
2. Tap the **"Generate Report"** button (Download icon).
3. A dialog will appear with several options:

### 🌍 Language Selection

- **English**: Generates a standard professional report.
- **Urdu**: Generates a report in Urdu, perfect for local customers!

### 📄 Format Selection

- **PDF Report**: A beautiful, premium document with your business name, heritage motifs, and a detailed table of records. Best for printing or formal sharing.
- **Text Summary**: A concise text message summarizing the balance and total milk. Best for a quick WhatsApp or SMS update.

## 2. Sharing the Report

Once the report is generated:

1. The **System Share Sheet** will open automatically.
2. Select **WhatsApp**, **Telegram**, **Email**, or your preferred sharing app.
3. Choose the customer and hit send!

## 3. Advanced PDF Features

Our PDF reports include:

- Your **Business Name** at the top.
- Detailed row-by-row breakdown of dates and quantities.
- A final **Outstanding Balance** clearly marked at the bottom.
