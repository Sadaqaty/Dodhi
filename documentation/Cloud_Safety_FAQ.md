# ☁️ Cloud Safety & FAQ

Your data is your business. We take its safety seriously.

## 1. How is my data protected?

Dodhi uses **zero-configuration Cloud Backup**. You don't need to sign in or set up anything.

- All your records are automatically backed up to your **Google Account** (the one you use for the Play Store).
- If you lose your phone, simply log into the same Google ID on your new phone and install Dodhi—your data will reappear instantly!

## 2. When does the backup happen?

Android backups your data silently in the background when:

1. Your phone is connected to **Wi-Fi**.
2. Your phone is **charging**.
3. Your phone is **idle** (usually at night).

## 3. How can I ensure my backup is active?

Go to your **Phone Settings > Google > Backup** and ensure "Backup by Google One" is turned **ON**.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I use the app without Internet?

**A:** Yes! Dodhi is **100% Offline-First**. You can record your entire day's run in the middle of a field with no signal. The app will sync your data to the cloud automatically once you get home and connect to Wi-Fi.

### Q: Does the app cost money?

**A:** No, Dodhi is a free tool designed to support our local milkmen.

### Q: Who can see my data?

**A:** Only you! Your database is stored locally on your device and in your private Google Cloud account. Even we (the developers) cannot see your records.
